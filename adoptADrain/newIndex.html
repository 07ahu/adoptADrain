Scavenger Hunt

Every drain on the map was found, photographed, and logged by someone in the neighborhood. Spot a painted drain, snap a photo, and add it to the map. Every clue you log helps the stormwater team see the bigger picture.

Adopt a Drain

Pick the drain you found and put your name on it. Adopting means checking in every few months, clearing leaves and trash, and reporting what you find. No mop required — just a trash bag and fifteen minutes.
